The Tulsa Road Information Feed

The Tulsa Road Information Feed (TRIF) is a Hackathon 2011 project of the Tulsa Web Developers, in partnership with INCOG.

Our data comes from several official sources:

  • The City of Tulsa's Live Traffic Report (specifically the XML feed)
  • The City of Tulsa's Road Closure Map
  • Oklahoma Department of Transportation's Traffic Advisories

We then use Google's Maps APIs to place the information on a map, and display live traffic information.

Developers Inforomation

There are other data representations available:

  • Atom feed of accidents and road closures with GeoRSS
  • JSON feed of accidents and road closures
  • Atom feed of accidents
  • JSON feed of accidents
  • Atom feed of road closures
  • JSON feed of road closures

If you have questions or would like to help with the project, join us at the Tulsa Web Developers monthly meeting, or try #tulsawebdevs on freenode. Our source code is at github, along with some additional documentation.
